About Disneyland Shanghai:

Shanghai Disneyland Park (Chinese: 上海迪士尼乐园) is a theme park located in Pudong, Shanghai, China, that is part of the Shanghai Disney Resort. The park is operated by Disney Parks, Experiences and Products and Shanghai Shendi Group, through a joint venture between The Walt Disney Company and Shendi. Construction began on April 8, 2011 The park opened on June 16, 2016. The park operated in its first half-year with a visitor attendance of 5.60 million guests
